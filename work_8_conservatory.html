A mobile room for daydreaming

Conservatory for Daydreamers

A room the size of a single tatami, with bubble tanks (filled with blue, viscous liquid, and air being discharged from the bottom to create bubbles) used in the walls and wheels attached for portability

PROJECT PERIOD: JUNE – JULY 2016
SIZE: 900×1800×1200
MATERIAL: OIL, ACRYLIC SHEETS, POLYURETHANE TUBES, AIR COMPRESSOR, WOOD (HINOKI)

ISSUE: Living in an environment where our minds are constantly overwhelmed by information, it is necessary for us to clear our brains once in a while. Taking a break away from the chaos of our everyday lives and entering a different state of mind is crucial to leading a humane life, as well as optimising your efficiency.


IDEA: By focusing on the organic transition of the bubbles, viewers can enter their subconscious and engage in a meditative trance.
